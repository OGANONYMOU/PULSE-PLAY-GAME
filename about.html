About PulsePay

PulsePay is an online community built by gamers, for gamers. Our goal is to keep players updated with the latest trends, news, and tournaments in mobile gaming — from CODM to eFootball and beyond.

We believe gaming connects people. That’s why we’re creating a space where players can interact, compete, and grow together. This is just the beginning — stay tuned for what’s next!
